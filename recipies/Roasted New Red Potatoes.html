Roasted New Red Potatoes

Description

Roasted red potatoes at their best — plain and simple. Red potatoes are tossed with olive oil, salt, and pepper, then roasted to perfection.

Ingridients

  • 3 pounds small red new potatoes, halved
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt and freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Place potatoes in a large bowl. Drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Toss to coat.
  3. Spread potatoes in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes, or until tender and golden brown.
