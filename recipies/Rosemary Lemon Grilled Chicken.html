Rosemary Lemon Grilled Chicken

Description

This simple, sensational marinade and sauce for grilled chicken is made with lemon,garlic, rosemary, and butter. Separate the marinade into thirds: 1/3 for marinating, 1/3 for basting, and 1/3 for topping.

Ingridients

  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup fresh rosemary
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 lemon, zested

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the rosemary, garlic, and lemon zest. Cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Remove from heat and let cool.
  3. Reserve 1/3 of the marinade for basting.
  4. Place chicken in a large resealable plastic bag. Pour the remaining marinade over the chicken. Seal and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or up to 4 hours.
  5. Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Remove chicken from marinade and discard marinade.
  6. Grill chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, basting with reserved marinade, until cooked through.
